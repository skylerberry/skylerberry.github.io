Position size is calculated as follows:

  • Position Size = Account Size × Risk Percentage
  • Shares to Purchase = Position Size ÷ (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price)
  • % of Account = (Position Size ÷ Account Size) × 100
  • Total Risk Amount = Account Size × Risk Percentage
  • R Multiple = (Target Price - Entry Price) ÷ (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price)
  • 5R Target Price = Entry Price + (5 × (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price))
